We have created easy search options in the drop-down menu for you to focus your results. Here’s a breakdown of what they do:

Everything: This search includes books, journal articles, media, and much more that are both in Musselman Library and beyond.

All items at Gettysburg: This search includes books, ebooks, and media that are available in Musselman Library. This search is similar to our previous MUSCAT search.

Special Collections: This search includes items like finding aids, manuscript collections, and rare books in Special Collections and College Archives.

Course Reserves: This search includes items that are on reserve for a specific course. You can use the course instructor’s name or course number to search for titles.
