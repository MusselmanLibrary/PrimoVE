Welcome to MUSCAT Plus!

MUSCAT Plus provides simple, one-stop searching for books and e-books, videos, articles, digital media, and more.

This catalog lookup station only searches materials held in Musselman Library. To search or view ebooks, electronic journal articles, or other online resources, please use one of the PCs in the library or your personal device.
